Choose Your Seattle Adventure

Welcome to our Adventure in Seattle game! If you choose to play, you'll be led on a virtual adventure around the city, to see whichever sights you'd like to see. You'll learn fun facts and trivia about each location along the way. At the end, you'll have a memento from your trip based on where you decided to go! Enjoy your time, hope you love Seattle!
